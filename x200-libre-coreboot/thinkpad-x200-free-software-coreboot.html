common issues why it's not working

If you can't think of any obvious reasons as to why nothing is showing up, it's very possible the ram is at fault. Having two different brands of ram dimm? is an obvious sign this is the issue (this caused it for me), but even if not, try booting with only one stick, or try any other available sticks you have. If that works, try two sticks again, changing the order.
